About

I’m Harris, a student at UC San Diego exploring the intersection of business and design. I love understanding how people think and then translating those insights into interfaces that feel intuitive.

In COGS 3, I’ve been building the foundations of front-end development—HTML, CSS, JavaScript—and using them to prototype small but meaningful experiences like planners, calculators, and games.

Outside of class, I’m interested in product strategy, creative projects, and adventures that bring a bit of joy to people’s everyday tools.

  • 2023 Started studying at UCSD, focusing on business economics while exploring a passion for photography.
